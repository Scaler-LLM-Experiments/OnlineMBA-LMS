## Getting Started

### Accessing the Assignment Portal

1. **Navigate to Dashboards**: From the main menu, click on "Dashboards"
2. **Select Assignment Dashboard**: Click on the "Assignment" dashboard card
3. **View All Assignments**: You'll see a list of all available assignments organized by status:
   - **Active**: Assignments currently open for submission
   - **Upcoming**: Assignments that will open soon
   - **Past Due**: Assignments with expired deadlines
   - **Completed**: Assignments you've already submitted

### Assignment List Overview

Each assignment card displays:
- **Assignment Title**: Name of the assignment
- **Subject**: The course or subject this assignment belongs to
- **Batch**: Your batch/cohort
- **Term**: Academic term (e.g., Term 1, Term 2)
- **Start Date & Time**: When the assignment opens
- **End Date & Time**: Submission deadline
- **Total Marks**: Maximum marks for this assignment
- **Status Badge**: Current status (Active, Upcoming, Completed, Past Due)

## Understanding Assignment Details

### Opening an Assignment

Click on any assignment card to view its complete details. The assignment details modal shows:

#### Header Information
- Assignment title and status
- Subject, batch, and term
- Start and end date/time with countdown timer
- Total marks
- Type (Individual or Group)

#### Assignment Content
- **Description**: Detailed instructions and requirements
- **Questions**: Up to 20 questions (some may be mandatory, marked with *)
- **Attachments**: Reference files or materials provided by the instructor
- **File Upload Requirements**:
  - Check if file uploads are **Mandatory** or **Optional**
  - This is clearly mentioned in the assignment details
  - **Always read this section carefully before starting your work**

## Individual Assignments

Individual assignments are completed and submitted by you alone.

### Completing an Individual Assignment

1. **Read Instructions**:
   - Carefully read the assignment description
   - **Check if file uploads are Mandatory or Optional** (clearly stated in assignment details)
   - Note which questions are mandatory (marked with *)

2. **Answer Questions**:
   - Type your answers in the provided text boxes
   - Questions marked with * are mandatory
   - You can format your text using line breaks

3. **Upload Files** (Check assignment requirements first):
   - **Only if required** by the assignment (check the file upload requirement mentioned in assignment details)
   - Click "Choose files" or drag and drop files into the upload area
   - You can upload multiple files
   - Large files will be uploaded with progress tracking

4. **Review**: Double-check all your answers and files before submitting
5. **Submit**: Click the "Submit Assignment" button

## Group Assignments

Group assignments require collaboration with other students and include additional steps.

### Step 1: Group Formation

**For FIRST-TIME Submission (Active Tab):**

**‚úÖ Good News: Your group is automatically formed!**

When you open a group assignment for the first time, the system **automatically fills**:

1. **Group Name** (Auto-filled):
   - Automatically set based on your subject grouping
   - Example: "Group A", "Team 1", "Section B"
   - This is pulled from admin-configured grouping in the system
   - **You can change it if needed** by typing a new name

2. **Group Members** (Auto-selected):
   - Your group members are **automatically selected** (checkboxes already ticked)
   - Based on your batch, subject, and group assignment
   - All students in your group will be pre-selected
   - **Important**: You won't see your own name in the list (you're automatically included)
   - **You can add or remove members** if needed by checking/unchecking boxes

**How to Modify Pre-filled Group (if needed):**
- **Change Group Name**: Simply type a new name in the field
- **Add Member**: Click on the dropdown, search by name/roll number, and select
- **Remove Member**: Click the X on their chip or uncheck their checkbox

**For VIEWING or UPDATING Submission (Completed Tab):**

- ‚úÖ **Group name is shown** - from your previous submission
- ‚úÖ **Group members are shown** - checkboxes show who you submitted with
- You can modify them during updates (only before deadline)

**Important Notes:**
- Group is auto-formed based on **admin configuration in "Group Members" sheet**
- If you see wrong members pre-selected, contact your instructor (may be configuration issue)
- Only ONE person in the group should create the INITIAL submission (even though everyone sees the same pre-filled group)
- The person creating the submission is automatically included (that's why you don't see yourself in the dropdown)
- Other group members will see the submission when they open the assignment in "Completed" tab
- All group members must be from the same batch

### Step 2: Complete Assignment Content

1. **Answer All Questions**: Fill in the required answers
2. **Upload Files**: Add any required attachments
3. **Group Member List**: Review the list of selected group members before proceeding

### Step 3: Peer Ratings (Optional During Submission)

If peer ratings are enabled for the assignment:

**During Initial Submission (Active Tab):**
- Peer ratings CAN be filled now OR after submission is made
- You'll see a section titled "Peer Rating & Remarks (Can be filled now or once submission is made)"
- You can choose to:
  - **Fill now**: Rate all your group members with remarks before submitting
  - **Fill later**: Skip this section and fill it after submission (even after deadline)

**Important Note Displayed:**
> ‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Peer ratings are mandatory. You must rate ALL group members with remarks to submit this assignment. Once submitted, ratings cannot be edited. Ratings can also be filled once the assignment deadline is expired.

**If you choose to fill ratings during submission:**
- If you start rating anyone, you must provide remarks for them
- You can rate some members or all members
- Partial ratings are allowed during initial submission

## Peer Ratings (Group Assignments)

Peer ratings allow you to evaluate your group members' contributions.

### When to Fill Peer Ratings

**Option 1: During Initial Submission (Optional)**
- Fill ratings in the "Active" tab before submitting
- You can skip and fill later

**Option 2: After Submission (Mandatory)**
- Go to the "Completed" tab
- View your submission details
- Fill the peer ratings section
- Click "Submit Peer Ratings" button

### How to Rate Group Members

For each group member (except yourself):

1. **Star Rating** (0.5 to 5 stars):
   - Click on stars to rate
   - Half stars available for more precise ratings
   - 5 stars = Excellent contribution
   - 1 star = Poor contribution

2. **Remarks** (Mandatory):
   - Type specific feedback about their contribution
   - Be honest and constructive
   - Examples: "Great communication and met all deadlines" or "Limited participation in discussions"

### Important Peer Rating Rules

- ‚úÖ You must rate ALL group members
- ‚úÖ Both rating AND remarks are mandatory
- ‚úÖ You cannot see or rate yourself
- ‚úÖ Once submitted, peer ratings CANNOT be edited
- ‚úÖ Ratings can be filled even after the assignment deadline expires
- ‚ùå You cannot skip any group member
- ‚ùå You cannot submit ratings without remarks

### Peer Rating Validation

**If you try to submit without completing ratings:**
- Error message: "Peer ratings are mandatory. Please rate all group members. Missing ratings for: [Names]"
- Error message: "Please provide remarks for all rated members: [Names]"

**Fix:** Go back and complete all missing ratings and remarks before resubmitting.

### Viewing Your Peer Feedback

After peer ratings are submitted by your group members, you can view:
- **Average Rating**: Your overall rating from all group members
- **Peer Remarks**: All comments and feedback from your group members
- This appears in the "Your Peer Feedback" section at the bottom of the submission

## Updating Submissions

### When Can You Update?

You can update your submission if:
- ‚úÖ The deadline has NOT passed
- ‚úÖ You have already submitted once
- ‚úÖ The assignment is still in "Active" status

### How to Update a Submission

1. **Click "Update Submission?" Button**: Located in the footer of the completed submission
2. **Confirmation Popup**:
   - Message: "This will let you modify your submission. Do you want to proceed?"
   - Click "Yes, Update"
3. **Review Pre-filled Data**:
   - All your previous answers are automatically shown
   - Previously uploaded files are displayed
   - **For group assignments**: Group name and members are automatically pre-filled and pre-selected
4. **Edit Your Work**:
   - Update answers to questions
   - Add or remove files
   - Modify group name or members (for group assignments - NOT RECOMMENDED unless necessary)
   - Update peer ratings (if not yet submitted)
5. **Click "Submit Updated Assignment"**: Save your changes
6. **Updated Timestamp**: Your submission timestamp updates to the latest submission time

### Update Restrictions

**You CANNOT update if:**
- ‚ùå Deadline has passed (Past Due status)
- ‚ùå Assignment is closed
- ‚ùå You're in the middle of uploading files (wait for uploads to complete)

**For Group Assignments:**
- Peer ratings must be completed before updating (if enabled)
- All group members must be rated
- Cannot edit peer ratings after they're submitted

## Best Practices

### üìù General Best Practices

1. **Start Early**
   - Don't wait until the last minute
   - Technical issues can occur near deadlines
   - Give yourself time to review and revise

2. **Read Instructions Carefully**
   - Review all questions before starting
   - Note mandatory vs optional fields
   - **Check assignment details to see if file uploads are Mandatory or Optional**
   - Don't upload files if not required
   - Understand the grading criteria

3. **Save Your Work Externally**
   - Keep a backup of your answers in a document
   - Save files locally before uploading
   - Don't rely solely on the portal for storage

4. **Check Your Internet Connection**
   - Stable internet required for file uploads
   - Large files may take time to upload
   - Don't close the browser during uploads

5. **Review Before Submitting**
   - Read through all your answers
   - Verify all files uploaded correctly
   - Check for typos and errors
   - Ensure all mandatory fields are filled

### üë• Group Assignment Best Practices

1. **Communication**
   - Discuss and agree on group name before creating submission
   - Coordinate who will create the initial submission
   - Share login details if needed (not recommended)
   - Keep everyone informed about submission status

2. **Group Formation**
   - Verify all group members are correctly selected
   - Double-check names and roll numbers
   - Add all members at once to avoid confusion
   - Confirm with group members they can see the submission

3. **Avoid Duplicate Submissions**
   - Only ONE person should create the submission
   - Others should check "Completed" tab to verify
   - Don't create multiple submissions for the same group
   - Coordinate updates carefully

4. **Peer Ratings Strategy**
   - Be honest and fair in your ratings
   - Provide constructive feedback in remarks
   - Rate based on actual contribution
   - Complete peer ratings promptly after submission
   - Don't wait until the last moment

5. **Group Member Changes**
   - Avoid changing group members after initial submission
   - If absolutely necessary, coordinate with instructor
   - Ensure all affected students are aware

### üì§ File Upload Best Practices

**‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Check Assignment First**
- Always check the assignment details to see if file uploads are **Mandatory** or **Optional**
- Don't waste time uploading files if they're not required
- If optional, only upload if you have supplementary materials

1. **File Naming**
   - Use clear, descriptive file names
   - Example: "Assignment1_Part2_JohnDoe.pdf"
   - Avoid special characters or spaces
   - Use underscores instead of spaces

2. **File Formats**
   - Use commonly accepted formats (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, JPG, PNG)
   - Verify the assignment accepts your file type
   - Convert files if necessary before uploading
   - Compress large files if possible

3. **File Size**
   - Check file size limits (if specified)
   - Large files take longer to upload
   - Wait for complete upload before submitting
   - Don't upload unnecessary large files

4. **Multiple Files**
   - Upload all required files at once
   - Label files clearly if submitting multiple parts
   - Organize files before starting upload
   - Verify each file uploaded successfully

5. **Upload Monitoring**
   - Watch the progress bar for each file
   - Wait for green checkmark (success)
   - Red X means error - try uploading again
   - Don't close browser during uploads

### ‚è∞ Deadline Management

1. **Submit Early**
   - Aim to submit at least 1 hour before deadline
   - Allows time for technical issues
   - Reduces stress and pressure
   - Gives time to fix any errors

2. **Set Personal Deadlines**
   - Set your deadline 24 hours before actual deadline
   - Use reminders and calendar alerts
   - Plan backward from deadline
   - Account for revision time

3. **Monitor Countdown Timer**
   - Check remaining time regularly
   - Red text = less than 24 hours remaining
   - Don't rely on countdown alone - note the actual deadline
   - Account for time zone differences if any

4. **After Deadline**
   - Cannot submit or update after deadline passes
   - Plan accordingly and don't miss it
   - Contact instructor immediately if you miss deadline
   - Peer ratings can still be completed after deadline

### üéØ Peer Rating Best Practices

1. **Timing**
   - Complete peer ratings within 24 hours of submission
   - Don't wait until someone reminds you
   - Set a reminder for yourself
   - Remember: It's mandatory!

2. **Rating Fairly**
   - Base ratings on actual contribution
   - Consider quality AND quantity of work
   - Account for communication and collaboration
   - Be consistent across all members

3. **Writing Remarks**
   - Be specific and constructive
   - Mention both strengths and areas for improvement
   - Use professional language
   - Avoid personal attacks or emotional comments
   - Examples:
     - ‚úÖ "Consistently met deadlines and contributed quality research"
     - ‚úÖ "Good communicator but could be more proactive in meetings"
     - ‚ùå "Lazy and didn't do anything"
     - ‚ùå "Best person ever, 10/10"

4. **Honesty and Integrity**
   - Don't give everyone 5 stars just to be nice
   - Don't rate based on friendship
   - Reflect actual contribution accurately
   - Remember: Ratings help instructors assess fairly

5. **Cannot Edit**
   - Think carefully before submitting
   - Review all ratings and remarks
   - Once submitted, CANNOT be changed
   - Contact instructor if you made a serious error

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### 1. "User not authenticated" Error
**Problem**: You're not properly logged in
**Solution**:
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support if issue persists

#### 2. File Upload Fails
**Problem**: File won't upload or shows error
**Solution**:
- Check file size (may be too large)
- Verify file format is supported
- Check internet connection
- Try uploading one file at a time
- Refresh the page and try again
- Try a different browser

#### 3. Wrong Group Members Pre-selected
**Problem**: See wrong students automatically selected or wrong group name
**Solution**:
- This is based on admin configuration in "Group Members" sheet
- Manually uncheck wrong members and add correct ones
- Change group name by typing new name in field
- If this keeps happening, contact instructor to fix configuration
- You can always override the pre-filled data

#### 4. Cannot Find Group Member
**Problem**: Student not appearing in group member dropdown
**Solution**:
- Check if they're already pre-selected (checkboxes at bottom)
- Verify they're in the same batch
- Check spelling of name/roll number in search
- Try searching by roll number instead of name
- Ensure they're enrolled in the course
- Contact instructor if student should be available

#### 5. "Peer ratings are mandatory" Error
**Problem**: Cannot submit because peer ratings incomplete
**Solution**:
- Rate ALL group members showing in the list
- Provide remarks for every rated member
- Ensure no member is left with 0 stars
- Verify remarks are not empty or just spaces

#### 6. "Deadline has passed" Error
**Problem**: Cannot submit or update after deadline
**Solution**:
- Check actual deadline date/time
- Account for time zone
- Contact instructor for late submission policy
- Peer ratings may still be possible even after deadline

#### 7. Submission Not Showing in Completed Tab
**Problem**: Submitted but can't see submission
**Solution**:
- Refresh the page
- Click on "Completed" tab explicitly
- Check if you're viewing the correct assignment
- Verify submission was successful (check for success message)
- Contact support if not visible after 5 minutes

#### 8. Cannot Update Submission
**Problem**: Update button not working or not visible
**Solution**:
- Check if deadline has passed
- Verify you've already submitted once
- For group assignments: Ensure peer ratings are completed
- Wait for any file uploads to complete
- Refresh page and try again

#### 9. Group Members Cannot See Submission
**Problem**: Other group members say they can't see the submission
**Solution**:
- Verify they're looking in "Completed" tab
- Confirm they were added to group members list
- Ask them to refresh the page
- Check if correct assignment is opened
- They may need to log out and log back in

#### 10. Wrong Group Members Added
**Problem**: Accidentally added wrong students to group
**Solution**:
- Note: Group is auto-filled based on configuration
- Remove incorrect members by unchecking their checkbox
- Add correct members from dropdown
- If already submitted: Use "Update Submission" feature
- Contact instructor if configuration needs fixing

#### 11. Large File Taking Too Long to Upload
**Problem**: File upload stuck or very slow
**Solution**:
- Check your internet speed
- Compress file if possible
- Try uploading during off-peak hours
- Upload one file at a time
- Consider splitting into smaller files if allowed
- Wait patiently - large files can take 10-15 minutes
